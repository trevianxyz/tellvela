This summer's EXPO 2017 in Astana has been one of the biggest events in Kazakhstan's history. More than 3 millions visitors from all over the world flocked to Astana for the event to learn about future energy and see star-studded entertainment.

Of course, we live in the age of social media, so there are tons of cool photos from EXPO 2017 all over the web. We went through Instagram and Twitter and found some of our favorite photos. Check them out below:
